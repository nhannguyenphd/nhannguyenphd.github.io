5-day Duke University Spirituality and Health Research Workshop 2024

It is truly an honor to study alongside my friends from around the world. They are remarkable individuals from Africa, Asia, Europe, and beyond. We represent institutions such as Stanford, Duke, Harvard, the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, and many other distinguished universities. What unites us is our shared passion for learning and our desire to understand the ever-changing world around us.

We respect one another deeply, and the bonds we've formed feel like family. These are the experiences I will cherish forever, especially from this period of my youth.

I am grateful to God, and I ask for His blessings on all of us.

North Carolina, August 12-17, 2024
