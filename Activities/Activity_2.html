UTSA Bridge Program 2024

Today saw the conclusion of the UTSA Bridge Program, in which first-generation undergraduate students were able to learn the fundamentals of the research process. Participants engaged in trainings, seminars, and workshops centered around career guidance and academic pathways, as well as the development of skills in quantitative analysis.
