Educational Journey Summary

Nhan D Nguyen began his academic journey in Vietnam, earning a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Psychology. He then pursued a Ph.D. in Statistics and Psychometrics at National Sun Yat-sen University in Taiwan, where he developed expertise in quantitative methods and cross-cultural research. Currently, he is continuing his academic path in the United States, having completed a Master's degree in Sociology at UTSA and now pursuing a second Ph.D. in Sociology of Religion at Baylor University in Texas.
